## How we work

Six phases. **4 to 16 weeks.** Every phase has an explicit exit criterion signed by both teams.

| # | Phase | Output | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| 01 | **Diagnosis** | Operational friction map | 1–2 weeks |
| 02 | **Hypothesis** | Impact thesis + architecture, signed scope | 1 week |
| 03 | **Discovery** | Navigable prototype, validated with real users | 2–3 weeks |
| 04 | **Build** | 2-week sprints with live demos, CI/CD from day one | 4–10 weeks |
| 05 | **Launch** | Cohort rollout, active monitoring, adoption program | 1–2 weeks |
| 06 | **Continuity** | Support and evolution under SLA | Ongoing |

Small teams of 3 to 5 people with full authority. No layers. No review meetings that dilute decisions.
